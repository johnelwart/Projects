Howdy, I'm Mitch


Hometown: Long Grove, Iowa

Major: Electrical Engineering, Math Minor

Interests: Boating, fishing, hunting, snowmobiling

One of my favorite hobbies is boating and fishing. Over the past few years I have enjoyed fixing up a variety of boat motors, finding new fishing spots, and taking a nice long cruise on my boat. Being out on the water is something I have always found so much joy in, so its safe to say that when the weather permits, I'll be on the water.
When it is too cold for boating, I enjoy snowmobiling. Every year, a group of us guys travel far up north to the Upper Peninsula of Michigan to a small logging town named Seney. We travel hundreds of miles by snowmobile, enjoying the deep powdery backcountry, freshly groomed trails and Great Lake views. Along with Michigan, trips are planned to Wisconsin and even out west to Colorado or Wyoming.
Time spent with family is something I value and enjoy dearly. Whether its a small dinner or a large gathering, I love being around my family. I recently proposed to my girlfriend of 4 years and will be married late next year! Along with family, comes my friends, who I also enjoy spending time with and continue to make wonderful memories with.
